Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel)
🔹 Project Description

This project focuses on analyzing coffee shop retail sales data to uncover meaningful insights that can improve business performance. The dashboard provides a clear understanding of customer behavior, sales trends, and product performance using Excel-based data analysis and visualization techniques.

📌 Problem Statement

The objective of this project is to analyze retail sales data and answer key business questions such as:

How do sales vary by day of the week and hour of the day?
What are the peak sales hours?
What is the monthly revenue trend?
How do different store locations perform?
What is the average order value per customer?
Which products are top-selling in terms of quantity and revenue?
How do product categories contribute to overall sales?

Tools & Technologies Used:-

Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables
Pivot Charts
Slicers & Filters
Data Cleaning & Transformation
Excel Functions (IF, SUM, AVERAGE, etc.)

Dashboard Features:-

Sales Trends Analysis (Hourly, Daily, Monthly)
Store-wise Performance Comparison
Product & Category Performance

KPI Metrics:
Total Sales
Total Orders
Average Order Value
Peak Sales Time Identification
Interactive Dashboard using slicers
📂 Dataset

The dataset contains transactional coffee shop sales data, including:

Date & Time
Store Location
Product Category & Type
Quantity Sold
Sales Amount

Key Insights:-
Peak sales occur during specific hours of the day (morning/afternoon trends).
Certain products contribute significantly to overall revenue.
Sales vary across store locations, highlighting high-performing outlets.
Weekends show different purchasing patterns compared to weekdays.
Product categories show varying contribution to total sales.

Business Impact:-

Helps in optimizing staffing during peak hours.
Supports inventory planning based on demand trends.
Identifies high-performing products for better marketing strategies.
Enables data-driven decision-making for business growth.
